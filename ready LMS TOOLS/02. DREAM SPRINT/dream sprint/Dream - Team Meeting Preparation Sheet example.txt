Team Meeting Preparation Sheet: Crafting the Company Dream

Module 1: Sprint 2
Sprint Title: Crafting the Company Dream
Purpose:
This team meeting aims to collaboratively develop a vivid, bold, and inspiring Dream for the company that reflects the future you want to create. The Dream should align with the company's core values, push boundaries, and serve as a guiding star for all strategic decisions and daily operations.
Preparation Instructions:
Please ensure that you have completed your individual preparation before the meeting. This includes:
1. Review the Brain Juice: Crafting Your Company Dream
Reflect on the key concepts and elements that resonate most with your vision for the company. Be ready to share your thoughts and insights with the team.
2. Complete the Company Dream Tool Exercise
Spend time on the individual reflection questions and be prepared to discuss your answers. This will form the foundation of our team discussion.
3. Familiarize Yourself with the Dream Visualization Tool
Consider the key words, images, and figures that you believe best represent our Dream. Think about how you would incorporate these into the Dream Board.
4. Review the Deep Dive Articles
Take note of any additional insights or ideas from the deep dive readings that you think are crucial to our Dream. Be prepared to integrate these into our discussions.
Agenda for the Meeting:
1. Do Better Than Last Time (5 minutes)
Review any feedback or learnings from previous sprints. Discuss how we can improve our collaboration and outcomes in this meeting.
2. Topic Presentation by Guru (15 minutes)
The Guru will present the key concepts from the Brain Juice and Dream tools, setting the stage for our discussion. This presentation will highlight the importance of crafting a bold, simple, and energizing Dream.
3. Discussion per Topic (30 minutes)
o Dream Reflection: Each team member will share their individual reflections from the Company Dream Tool exercise. Discuss the common themes and differences.
o Dream Visualization: Collaboratively select key words, images, and figures for the Dream Board. Ensure these elements are aligned with our company's core values and vision for the future.
4. Decision per Topic (20 minutes)
o Finalizing the Dream: Synthesize the input from the discussion into a cohesive, one-page narrative. This will be the draft version of our Company Dream.
o Dream Board Elements: Decide on the visuals and figures that will be included in the Dream Board. Assign tasks for creating and displaying the board.
5. Reflection Time (10 minutes)
Reflect on the decisions made during the meeting. Discuss any potential challenges in communicating and living the Dream within the company. Consider next steps and how we can ensure the Dream remains a central part of our company's culture.
Total Meeting Time: 90 minutes

This preparation sheet is designed to ensure that the team meeting is focused, productive, and aligned with the Fast Track principles. By coming prepared and following this structured approach, we can craft a Dream that inspires and drives our company forward.
