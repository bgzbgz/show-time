# Sprint 17: Product Development - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Design and develop products that deliver on your value proposition using lean, customer-centric methodologies.

## Decision to Make
What features and capabilities must we build to deliver our value proposition effectively?

## Key Concepts
- **Product-Market Fit**: Building what customers actually need
- **Lean Development**: MVP, build-measure-learn loops
- **Feature Prioritization**: Must-have vs. nice-to-have

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - VP testing insights
2. Reading Materials - Product development frameworks
3. Think and Do - Define product roadmap and MVP
4. Team Session - Align on development priorities
