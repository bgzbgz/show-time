# Sprint 26: Employer Branding - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Develop a compelling employer brand to attract, engage, and retain top talent.

## Decision to Make
What is our employee value proposition, and how do we position ourselves as an employer of choice?

## Key Concepts
- **Employer Value Proposition (EVP)**: Why people should work here
- **Talent Attraction**: Recruiting and hiring strategies
- **Employee Experience**: Onboarding, development, retention

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Org redesign foundation
2. Reading Materials - Employer branding frameworks
3. Think and Do - Define EVP and recruitment strategy
4. Team Session - Launch employer branding initiatives

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 03 (Values), Sprint 25 (Org Redesign)
- Feeds into: Sprint 27 (Agile Teams), Sprint 30 (Implementation)
