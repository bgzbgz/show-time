# Sprint 06: Cash - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Master cash flow management and financial planning to ensure sustainable growth and avoid financial distress.

## Key Concepts

- **Working Capital**: Managing receivables, payables, and inventory
- **Financial Planning**: Creating sustainable financial models
