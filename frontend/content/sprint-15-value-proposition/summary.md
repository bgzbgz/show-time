# Sprint 15: Value Proposition - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Craft a compelling value proposition that clearly articulates why customers should choose your solution.

## Decision to Make
What unique value do we deliver to our target customers that competitors cannot match?

## Key Concepts
- **Value Proposition Canvas**: Jobs, pains, gains, products, pain relievers, gain creators
- **Differentiation**: Unique selling proposition vs. competition
- **Value Articulation**: Clear, compelling messaging

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Target segment understanding
2. Reading Materials - Value proposition frameworks
3. Think and Do - Complete Value Proposition Canvas
4. Team Session - Refine and validate value proposition

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 14 (Target Segment)
- Feeds into: Sprint 16 (VP Testing), Sprint 19 (Brand Marketing), Sprint 21 (Route to Market)
