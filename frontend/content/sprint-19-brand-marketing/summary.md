# Sprint 19: Brand Marketing - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Build a strong brand identity and marketing strategy that attracts and engages your target customers.

## Decision to Make
How do we position our brand and what marketing channels will most effectively reach our target segment?

## Key Concepts
- **Brand Identity**: Mission, vision, personality, visual identity
- **Marketing Channels**: Digital, content, social, events, partnerships
- **Marketing Funnel**: Awareness, consideration, conversion, retention

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Pricing foundation
2. Reading Materials - Brand and marketing frameworks
3. Think and Do - Define brand positioning and channel strategy
4. Team Session - Create integrated marketing plan

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 15 (Value Proposition), Sprint 18 (Pricing)
- Feeds into: Sprint 21 (Route to Market), Sprint 26 (Employer Branding)
