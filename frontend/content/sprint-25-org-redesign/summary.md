# Sprint 25: Org Redesign - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Redesign organizational structure to support strategy execution and enable growth.

## Decision to Make
What organizational structure, roles, and reporting lines will best support our strategic objectives?

## Key Concepts
- **Organizational Design**: Structure follows strategy
- **Role Definition**: Clear accountabilities and decision rights
- **Org Chart**: Functional, divisional, matrix, or hybrid structures

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Strategic fit insights
2. Reading Materials - Organizational design principles
3. Think and Do - Design optimal org structure
4. Team Session - Finalize new structure and transition plan
