# Sprint 21: Route to Market - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Define the optimal channels and distribution strategy to reach customers and deliver your product/service.

## Decision to Make
How will we sell and distribute our products/services to reach our target customers most effectively?

## Key Concepts
- **Distribution Channels**: Direct, indirect, hybrid, omnichannel
- **Sales Model**: Inside sales, field sales, channel partners, self-service
- **Go-to-Market Strategy**: Launch plan and customer acquisition approach

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Customer service foundation
2. Reading Materials - Distribution and sales frameworks
3. Think and Do - Design route-to-market strategy
4. Team Session - Finalize GTM plan and channel strategy

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 15 (Value Proposition), Sprint 19 (Brand Marketing), Sprint 20 (Customer Service)
- Feeds into: Sprint 22 (Core Activities), Sprint 30 (Implementation)
