# Sprint 13: Segmentation - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Divide the market into distinct customer segments to focus marketing and product development efforts effectively.

## Decision to Make
How should we segment our market to identify the most attractive customer groups to target?

## Key Concepts
- **Segmentation Criteria**: Demographics, psychographics, behavior, needs
- **Segment Evaluation**: Size, growth, profitability, accessibility
- **Segment Selection**: Choosing where to compete

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Market size foundation
2. Reading Materials - Segmentation frameworks
3. Think and Do - Create market segmentation model
4. Team Session - Evaluate and prioritize segments

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 12 (Market Size)
- Feeds into: Sprint 14 (Target Segment), Sprint 15 (Value Proposition)
