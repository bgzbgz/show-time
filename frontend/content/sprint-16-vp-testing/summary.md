# Sprint 16: VP Testing - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Validate your value proposition through systematic customer testing and feedback before full market launch.

## Decision to Make
Does our value proposition resonate with target customers, and what refinements do we need to make?

## Key Concepts
- **Hypothesis Testing**: Formulating testable assumptions
- **Customer Interviews**: Gathering qualitative feedback
- **MVP Testing**: Minimum viable product validation approaches

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Value proposition foundation
2. Reading Materials - Testing methodologies
3. Think and Do - Design and conduct VP tests
4. Team Session - Analyze results and iterate

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 15 (Value Proposition)
- Feeds into: Sprint 17 (Product Development), Sprint 18 (Pricing), Sprint 19 (Brand Marketing)
