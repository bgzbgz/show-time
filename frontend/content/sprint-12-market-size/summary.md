# Sprint 12: Market Size - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Analyze total available market and profit pools to understand the full scope of opportunity and inform strategic decisions.

## Decision to Make
What is the true size and profitability of our target market, and where should we focus our efforts?

## Key Concepts
- **Market Size Analysis**: TAM, SAM, SOM calculations
- **Profit Pool Analysis**: Understanding margins and profit distribution
- **Supply Chain Mapping**: Identifying inefficiencies and opportunities

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Meeting foundation
2. Reading Materials - Market analysis methodologies
3. Think and Do - Calculate market size and profit pools
4. Team Session - Align on market opportunity assessment

- Feeds into: Sprint 13 (Segmentation), Sprint 14 (Target Segment), Sprint 15 (Value Proposition)
