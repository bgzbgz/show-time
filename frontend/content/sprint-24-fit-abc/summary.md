# Sprint 24: Fit ABC - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Conduct comprehensive strategic fit analysis across Activities, Brand, and Customers to ensure alignment.

## Decision to Make
Are our activities, brand, and customer focus aligned to create sustainable competitive advantage?

## Key Concepts
- **Activity Fit**: Alignment of value chain activities
- **Brand Fit**: Consistency between brand promise and delivery
- **Customer Fit**: Match between offerings and customer needs

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Process foundation
2. Reading Materials - Strategic fit and alignment frameworks
3. Think and Do - Complete comprehensive fit analysis
4. Team Session - Identify and address misalignments

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 05 (Fit), Sprint 19 (Brand), Sprint 22 (Core Activities)
- Feeds into: Sprint 25 (Org Redesign), Sprint 30 (Implementation)
