# Sprint 20: Customer Service - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Design exceptional customer service and support systems that drive satisfaction, retention, and advocacy.

## Decision to Make
What level and type of customer service will we provide, and how will we organize to deliver it?

## Key Concepts
- **Service Standards**: Response times, resolution rates, satisfaction targets
- **Support Channels**: Phone, email, chat, self-service, community
- **Customer Success**: Proactive support and value realization

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Marketing foundation
2. Reading Materials - Customer service excellence frameworks
3. Think and Do - Design service model and standards
4. Team Session - Implement customer service strategy

- Requires: Sprint 19 (Brand Marketing)
- Feeds into: Sprint 21 (Route to Market), Sprint 23 (Processes)
