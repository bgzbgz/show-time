# Sprint 07: Energy - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Optimize personal and organizational energy to maximize productivity, creativity, and sustainable performance.

## Decision to Make
How can we manage our energy (not just time) to achieve peak performance individually and as a team?

## Key Concepts
- **Energy Management**: Physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual energy
- **Peak Performance**: Aligning work with natural energy rhythms
- **Sustainable Productivity**: Building practices for long-term high performance

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Financial foundation
2. Reading Materials - Energy management science
3. Think and Do - Personal energy audit and optimization
4. Team Session - Create team energy practices

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 06 (Cash)
- Feeds into: Sprint 09 (Focus), Sprint 10 (Performance), Sprint 11 (Meeting Rhythm)
