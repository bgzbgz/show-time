# Sprint 14: Target Segment - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Select and deeply understand your ideal customer segment to focus all go-to-market efforts.

## Decision to Make
Which specific customer segment will we target, and what are their critical needs and behaviors?

## Key Concepts
- **Segment Selection**: Choosing the best-fit target segment
- **Customer Deep Dive**: Understanding jobs-to-be-done, pain points, gains
- **Buyer Personas**: Creating detailed customer profiles

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Segmentation foundation
2. Reading Materials - Target customer frameworks
3. Think and Do - Define target segment and create personas
4. Team Session - Align on ideal customer profile

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 13 (Segmentation)
- Feeds into: Sprint 15 (Value Proposition), Sprint 16 (VP Testing), All GTM sprints
