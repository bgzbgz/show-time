# Sprint 08: Goals - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Master strategic goal setting and prioritization to ensure all efforts align with and drive toward the company's most important objectives.

## Decision to Make
What are our clear, measurable goals for the next quarter/year, and how do we prioritize actions to achieve them?

## Key Concepts
- **Goal Clarity**: Defining measurable, aligned objectives
- **Prioritization Frameworks**: 80/20 rule, impact vs. effort analysis
- **Quarterly Planning**: Breaking goals into actionable quarterly plans

## Tool Sections
1. Goal Setting - Define clear, measurable objectives
2. Prioritization - Apply frameworks to identify high-impact tasks
3. Planning - Create actionable quarterly plans
4. Alignment - Ensure team-wide commitment and clarity

- Requires: Sprint 02 (Dream), Sprint 06 (Cash), Sprint 07 (Energy)
- Feeds into: Sprint 09 (Focus), Sprint 10 (Performance), All execution sprints
