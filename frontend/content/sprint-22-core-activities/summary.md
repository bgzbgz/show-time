# Sprint 22: Core Activities - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Identify and optimize the critical activities that create value and differentiate your business.

## Decision to Make
What are the essential activities we must excel at, and which should we outsource or eliminate?

## Key Concepts
- **Value Chain Analysis**: Identifying primary and support activities
- **Core Competencies**: Activities that create competitive advantage
- **Make vs. Buy**: Deciding what to do in-house vs. outsource

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - GTM foundation
2. Reading Materials - Value chain and activity frameworks
3. Think and Do - Map and prioritize core activities
4. Team Session - Align on activity focus and resource allocation

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 09 (Focus), Sprint 21 (Route to Market)
- Feeds into: Sprint 23 (Processes), Sprint 25 (Org Design), Sprint 28 (Digitalization)
