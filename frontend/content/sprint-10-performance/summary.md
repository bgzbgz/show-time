# Sprint 10: Performance - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Establish performance management systems and metrics that drive accountability and continuous improvement.

## Decision to Make
How do we measure, track, and improve performance at individual, team, and company levels?

## Key Concepts
- **KPI Development**: Identifying key performance indicators
- **Performance Tracking**: Building dashboards and review systems
- **Accountability Systems**: Creating clear ownership and consequences

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Focus foundation
2. Reading Materials - Performance management frameworks
3. Think and Do - Define KPIs and tracking mechanisms
4. Team Session - Implement performance review cadence

- Requires: Sprint 08 (Goals), Sprint 09 (Focus)
- Feeds into: Sprint 11 (Meeting Rhythm), Sprint 23 (Processes)
