# Sprint 09: Focus - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Develop laser focus by eliminating distractions and concentrating efforts on the vital few priorities that drive results.

## Decision to Make
What will we say NO to, and how do we maintain unwavering focus on our top priorities?

## Key Concepts
- **Focus Discipline**: Saying no to good opportunities for great ones
- **Distraction Elimination**: Identifying and removing focus killers
- **Deep Work**: Creating conditions for sustained concentration

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Goal alignment
2. Reading Materials - Focus and deep work principles
3. Think and Do - Focus audit and distraction elimination
4. Team Session - Create team focus commitments

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 08 (Goals), Sprint 07 (Energy)
- Feeds into: Sprint 10 (Performance), Sprint 22 (Core Activities)
