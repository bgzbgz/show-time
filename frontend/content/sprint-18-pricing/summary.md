# Sprint 18: Pricing - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Develop a pricing strategy that captures value, aligns with customer willingness to pay, and supports business goals.

## Decision to Make
What pricing model and price points will maximize both customer value and company profitability?

## Key Concepts
- **Pricing Models**: Subscription, usage-based, value-based, tiered
- **Price Setting**: Cost-plus, competitive, value-based approaches
- **Pricing Psychology**: Anchoring, bundling, framing

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Product foundation
2. Reading Materials - Pricing frameworks and strategies
3. Think and Do - Design pricing model and test scenarios
4. Team Session - Finalize pricing strategy

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 17 (Product Development), Sprint 06 (Cash)
- Feeds into: Sprint 19 (Brand Marketing), Sprint 21 (Route to Market), Sprint 30 (Implementation)
