# Sprint 05: Fit - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Analyze and optimize the strategic fit between your resources, capabilities, and market opportunities.

## Decision to Make
Where is our company best positioned to win, and what adjustments do we need to maximize our competitive advantage?

## Key Concepts
- **Strategic Fit Analysis**: Assessing alignment between capabilities and opportunities
- **Competitive Positioning**: Understanding where you can win
- **Resource Optimization**: Allocating resources for maximum impact

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Team foundation
2. Reading Materials - Strategic fit frameworks
3. Think and Do - Conduct fit analysis exercise
4. Team Session - Define strategic positioning

- Requires: Sprint 04 (Team)
- Feeds into: Sprint 12 (Market Size), Sprint 24 (Fit ABC), All market sprints
