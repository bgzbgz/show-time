# Sprint 01: Know Thyself - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Empower participants to understand their personal strengths, values, and purpose to lead with authenticity and clarity.

## Decision to Make
What are my core strengths and values, and how do they guide my professional decisions and leadership?

## Key Concepts
- **StrengthsFinder**: Identifying and leveraging personal strengths
- **Values Clarification**: Understanding core beliefs that drive behavior
- **Purpose Discovery**: Connecting personal mission to professional contribution

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint and Learnings
2. Reading Materials - Self-awareness foundations
3. Think and Do - Strengths and values exercises
4. Team Session - Sharing insights and alignment

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 00 (WOOP preparation)
- Feeds into: Sprint 03 (Values), Sprint 08 (Goals)
