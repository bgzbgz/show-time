# Sprint 11: Meeting Rhythm - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Design and implement an effective meeting cadence that drives alignment, decision-making, and accountability without wasting time.

## Decision to Make
What is our optimal meeting structure and rhythm to maximize productivity and minimize wasted time?

## Key Concepts
- **Meeting Cadence**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly rhythms
- **Meeting Types**: Tactical vs. strategic, different formats for different purposes
- **Meeting Effectiveness**: Agendas, facilitation, and follow-through

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Performance systems
2. Reading Materials - Meeting effectiveness principles
3. Think and Do - Design meeting architecture
4. Team Session - Implement new meeting rhythm
