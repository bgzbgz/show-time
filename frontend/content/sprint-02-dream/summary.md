# Sprint 02: Dream - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Craft a compelling company Dream that serves as the North Star, guiding strategic direction and aligning the entire organization.

## Decision to Make
What is our company's bold 10-year vision that will energize the team and drive long-term success?

## Key Concepts
- **Company Dream**: Vivid, bold story defining the 10-year destination
- **Vision Alignment**: Connecting personal values to company aspirations
- **Strategic North Star**: Using the Dream to guide all major decisions

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Reflection on self-knowledge
2. Reading Materials - Dream crafting frameworks
3. Think and Do - Draft company Dream statement
4. Team Session - Align on shared vision

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 01 (Know Thyself)
- Feeds into: Sprint 03 (Values), Sprint 08 (Goals), All strategic sprints
