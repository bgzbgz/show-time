# Sprint 04: Team - Quick Reference

## Purpose
Build a high-performing team structure with clear roles, accountability, and effective collaboration mechanisms.

## Decision to Make
How do we structure our team and define roles to maximize performance and achieve our goals?

## Key Concepts
- **Team Structure**: Organizing for optimal performance and accountability
- **Role Clarity**: Defining responsibilities and decision rights
- **Collaboration Mechanisms**: Establishing effective team interaction patterns

## Tool Sections
1. Review of Last Sprint - Values foundation
2. Reading Materials - High-performance team frameworks
3. Think and Do - Map team structure and roles
4. Team Session - Align on structure and accountabilities

## Dependencies
- Requires: Sprint 03 (Values)
- Feeds into: Sprint 11 (Meeting Rhythm), Sprint 25 (Org Design), Sprint 27 (Agile Teams)
