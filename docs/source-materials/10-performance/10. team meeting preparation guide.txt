[MODULE:MASTER THE CORE ELEMENT OF PERFORMANCE - SPRINT:PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS AND ACCOUNTABILITY]
TEAM MEETING PREPARATION GUIDE 














[01/06]
meeting Purpose
This meeting is critical to establishing two foundational systems that will drive your team’s success:
1. Performance analysis system
A robust framework to measure individual and team outputs effectively.
2. Accountability and consequences system
A structured plan ensuring clarity in roles, consistent follow-through, and clear repercussions for actions.
By the end of this meeting, you will have clear agreements on who, what, when, and how for both systems, ensuring alignment and readiness for immediate implementation.

[02/06]
What to Prepare

1. Review 
The Sprint Brain Juice document on Performance analysis and accountability. 
Fast Track guides on the Theory of Constraints and 5 Whys for root cause analysis. 
The Accountability and Consequences framework.
2. Complete individual tools 
Individual reflection on tasks completed or not completed last week, reasons for gaps, and suggested improvements.
Draft a proposed accountability checklist for your area of responsibility.
3. Discussion points
Come prepared with insights on how your role impacts performance metrics. 
Suggestions for key accountability practices specific to your function. 

[03/06]
Pre-Mortem Instructions
Potential issues
Lack of clarity in defining tasks or metrics. 
Resistance to accountability measures.
Overestimation of resources or under-allocation of ownership. 
Preventive strategies
Focus on simplicity when defining metrics and tasks.
Facilitate open discussion to address concerns about accountability.
Use real data to back decisions on task assignments and consequences.

[04/06]
Meeting Agenda
team meeting
Total duration: 60 minutes

1. Objective setting (5 min)
Brief recap of sprint goals and the meeting's objectives.
2. Setting up the performance analysis system (25 min)
Review the proposed system structure (tasks done/not done, reasons for gaps, and improvement commitments).
Discuss individual and team-level dashboards. 
Agree on the timeline and ownership for setup.
3. Planning the accountability and consequences system (25 min)
Review the Accountability Checklist and key practices. 
Align on the consequences framework (rewards for success, corrective actions for gaps). 
Assign roles for implementing and monitoring the system
4. Action plan and closing (5 min)
Confirm “Who, What, When” for all agreed actions. 
Address final questions and summarize key takeaways. 
[05/06]
checklist 

Setting up the performance analysis system
Are the tasks and metrics defined clearly for all team members? 
Is there a binary approach (done/not done) to task evaluation? 
Are dashboards designed to track individual and team metrics?
Accountability and consequences system:
Are roles and responsibilities for accountability clearly assigned? 
Is the consequences framework transparent and fair?
Are timelines for setup and execution realistic and agreed upon?
[06/06]
Final Reminders
Focus on precision: Avoid vague metrics or roles—everything must be specific and measurable. 
Engage fully: Every participant’s input is critical for success.
Follow-up: Ensure all pre-meeting preparation is complete to maximize productivity during discussions.
