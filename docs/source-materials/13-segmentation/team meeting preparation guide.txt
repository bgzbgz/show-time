meeting Purpose

[03/05]
Pre-Mortem
Potential issues
Unclear criteria for segment ranking
Risk: Misalignment on how to prioritize size, profitability, trend, and fit. 
Overanalysis paralysis
Risk: Getting bogged down in data precision, delaying decision-making. 
Derailing discussions
Risk: Team debates straying from the agenda, reducing focus.
Preventive strategies
Alignment workshop
Strategy: Agree on weighted criteria for the attractiveness index before ranking. 
70% rule
Strategy: Emphasize actionable insights over perfect data.
Facilitator intervention
Strategy: Use time checks and redirect questions to maintain focus.

[04/05]
Meeting Agenda
team meeting
Total duration: 90 minutes

1. Opening remarks (5 min)
Overview of sprint goals and agenda.
2. Behavioral segmentation insights (20 min)
Share and discuss individual findings.
Validate and refine behavioral clusters.
3. Attractiveness index refinement (25 min)
Agree on weighted criteria and rankings.
Prioritize high-value segments.
4. Market share targets and end game (25 min)
Align on market share goals and next steps.
Define the 3–5-year vision for the selected segments.
5. Action plan and closing (15 min)
Summarize key decisions and assign responsibilities.
Set deadlines for follow-up tasks.
