[MODULE:ORGANISATIONAL STRUCTURE - SPRINT:FIT & ABC ANALYSIS]
TEAM MEETING PREPARATION GUIDE 














[01/03]
Purpose of the Meeting
This meeting is crucial for ensuring that we have the right people doing the right work in the right way. We’ll use the FIT and ABC frameworks to evaluate our teams strategically. Your honest insights will help us strengthen our bench, identify development needs, and address misalignment early.

[02/03]
What to Prepare Before the Meeting
Before the meeting:
Review your Individual Preparation Sheet 
Reflect on each of your direct reports using the FIT and ABC logic
Identify team members you are unsure about. Think about - what makes you hesitate? 
Be ready to contribute to open and respectful discussions

[03/03]
Meeting Agenda
Proposed Meeting agenda
Total duration: 90 minutes

1. Do Better Than Last Time (10 min)
Reflect on people-related lessons and missed opportunities

2. Topic Presentation from Guru (15 min)
Overview of FIT & ABC tools and how to apply them

3. Discussion Per Topic (30 min)
Share individual insights and observations—map team on the tools

4. Decision Per Topic (20 min)
Agree on top actions: who needs support, role changes, or tough calls

5. Reflection Time (15 min)
Summarize key takeaways and align on next steps
